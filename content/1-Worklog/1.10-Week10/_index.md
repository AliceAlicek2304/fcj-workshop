### Week 10 Objectives:

* Learn about AWS S3 (Simple Storage Service) and CloudFront (CDN) services.
* Understand S3 bucket creation, permissions, and storage classes.
* Practice uploading files to S3 and configuring public/private access.
* Learn about CloudFront distributions to deliver content globally.
* Understand versioning, lifecycle policies, and basic security best practices.
